# Week 50 Quiz — Peer Review and Beta Test — Ages 10–13

> *10 code-focused, CBE-aligned questions for Week 50: Peer Review and Beta Test.*

## Questions

1. What is peer review?
2. What is one kind thing to say in a review?
3. What is one helpful suggestion to give?
4. Why should you be kind when reviewing?
5. What is a beta test?
6. How do you record feedback?
7. Name one thing to check when testing a classmate's game.
8. What should you do with feedback?
9. How do you respond to feedback?
10. Which CBE strand links to peer review?

## Answer Key

1. When classmates look at each other's work and give feedback.
2. I like how you...
3. You could add...
4. It helps the other person learn.
5. Letting others try your project before the final version.
6. Write it down.
7. Does it start? Are the controls clear?
8. Use it to improve your project.
9. Listen, ask questions, and decide what to change.
10. Communication / Collaboration.
