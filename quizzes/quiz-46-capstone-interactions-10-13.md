## Questions

5. What happens when the shark hits a plastic bag?
6. Why are interactions important for gameplay?
7. Name one sound or effect you could add to an interaction.
8. How do you make an interaction repeatable?
9. What is player feedback?
10. Which CBE strand links to interactions?

1. When the player or objects affect each other.
2. Shark eats fish, player collects data, shark hits reef.
3. Check if their positions are close.
4. if shark.distance_to(fish) < 30:
    score += 1
5. Score goes down or health decreases.
6. They make the game fun and meaningful.
7. Splash sound or flash effect.
8. Reset the object after it is used.
9. Signals that tell the player something happened.
