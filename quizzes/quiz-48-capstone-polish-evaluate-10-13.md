# Week 48 Quiz — Capstone Build 4 — Polish & Evaluate — Ages 10–13

> *10 code-focused, CBE-aligned questions for Week 48: Capstone Build 4 — Polish & Evaluate.*

## Questions

1. What does 'polish' mean in a project?
2. Name two things you can polish in your capstone.
3. How do you know if your game is fun?
4. What is a bug that you fixed this week?
5. Why is it important to test your game with others?
6. Write one improvement you made.
7. What is a final check before sharing?
8. How do you explain your game to a player?
9. Name one thing you are proud of in your capstone.
10. Which CBE strand links to evaluation?

## Answer Key

1. Improving details to make it better.
2. Add instructions, fix bugs, improve colours.
3. Ask someone to play it and watch their reaction.
4. [Teacher checks: bug]
5. They find problems you missed.
6. [Teacher checks: improvement]
7. Make sure it runs without errors.
8. Tell them the goal and controls.
9. [Teacher checks: pride point]
10. Learning to Learn / Communication.
