# Week 25 Quiz — Data Ethics — Keep Wildlife Safe — Ages 10–13

> *10 code-focused, CBE-aligned questions for Week 25: Data Ethics — Keep Wildlife Safe.*

## Questions

1. What is private data?
2. Give one example of private wildlife data.
3. Why should you not share passwords?
4. What should you do before posting a photo of someone online?
5. Why is it important to give credit when using someone else's data?
6. What is one rule for safe online behaviour?
7. Who owns data collected by a research project?
8. What does 'consent' mean?
9. Why should wildlife locations stay private?
10. Which CBE strand links to data ethics?

## Answer Key

1. Information that should not be shared with everyone.
2. Exact nesting beach location.
3. Others could pretend to be you.
4. Ask permission.
5. It respects their work.
6. Do not share personal information.
7. The people or organisation that collected it, with community consent.
8. Permission to use data or images.
9. To protect animals from poaching or disturbance.
10. Citizenship.
