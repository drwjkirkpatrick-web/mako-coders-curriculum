# Week 51 Quiz — Final Presentation Rehearsal — Ages 14–17

> *10 code-focused, CBE-aligned questions for Week 51: Final Presentation Rehearsal.*

## Questions

1. Write an outline for a 5-minute capstone presentation.
2. What is a backup plan for a live demo?
3. How do you tailor a presentation to different audiences?
4. Explain the rule of three in presentations.
5. How do you handle nerves before presenting?
6. What is a call-to-action in a final presentation?
7. How do you use slides without reading from them?
8. What questions should you expect from judges?
9. How would you present code without overwhelming the audience?
10. Map presentation skills to CBE Communication outcomes.

## Answer Key

1. Hook, problem, solution/demo, technical highlights, impact, Q&A.
2. Have screenshots or a short video ready.
3. Use different examples and depth based on who is listening.
4. People remember ideas better in groups of three.
5. Practice, breathe, and focus on the message.
6. A clear request, e.g., 'Try the game and share one ocean fact.'
7. Put keywords on slides and speak from notes.
8. Why this project, how it works, what you learned, next steps.
9. Show one key function and explain it in plain language.
10. Learners communicate ideas clearly and confidently.
