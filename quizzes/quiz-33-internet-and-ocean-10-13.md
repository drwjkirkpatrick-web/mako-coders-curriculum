# Week 33 Quiz — Internet and the Ocean — Ages 10–13

> *10 code-focused, CBE-aligned questions for Week 33: Internet and the Ocean.*

## Questions

1. What is the internet?
2. What does a web browser do?
3. Name two things you can do on the internet.
4. What is a URL?
5. Why is the internet useful for ocean research?
6. What is a website?
7. How can you tell if a website is trustworthy?
8. What does 'offline' mean?
9. Name one Kenyan ocean data source on the internet.
10. Which CBE strand links to the internet?

## Answer Key

1. A global network of connected computers.
2. It shows web pages.
3. Send email, watch videos, research.
4. The address of a web page.
5. Scientists can share data quickly.
6. A collection of web pages on the internet.
7. Check the author and source.
8. Not connected to the internet.
9. IFAW, KWS, WCS reports.
10. Networks / Digital Literacy.
