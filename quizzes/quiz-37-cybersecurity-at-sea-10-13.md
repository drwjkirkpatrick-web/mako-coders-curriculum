# Week 37 Quiz — Cybersecurity at Sea — Ages 10–13

> *10 code-focused, CBE-aligned questions for Week 37: Cybersecurity at Sea.*

## Questions

1. What is a password?
2. Why should passwords be hard to guess?
3. Give one example of a strong password.
4. What is phishing?
5. How do you know an email might be phishing?
6. What should you do if you see a suspicious link?
7. Why is it important to log out of shared computers?
8. What does 'private' mean online?
9. Name one way to protect a school coding account.
10. Which CBE strand links to cybersecurity?

## Answer Key

1. A secret word or phrase that proves who you are.
2. So other people cannot access your account.
3. A phrase like BlueTurtle$2026!
4. A fake message that tries to trick you into giving information.
5. It asks for passwords or has strange links.
6. Do not click it; tell an adult.
7. So the next person cannot use your account.
8. Only you or approved people can see it.
9. Use a strong password and do not share it.
10. Security.
